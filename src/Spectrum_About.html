ABOUT SPECTRUM

Natalia (Vocals)

Natlia was born and raised in St. Petersburg Russia. She brings a unique style to Spectrum and her versatility is unrivaled. Whether she is singing a powerful ballad, pop mega hit, or country classic, she is a true crowd pleaser catering to all!

Scott (Guitar)

Scott was born and raised in Virginia. He recently graduated from George Washington University with a Bachelors in Psychical Therapy. He is extremely passionate about music though and continues to shred the guitar whenever possible.

Ramon (Vocals)

Ramon grew up in Maryland and brings his unique Latin flavor to Spectrum’s vast repertoire. Additionally his charisma and high energy ensure that the party never stops when he is on stage!

Ben (Keys/Bass)

Ben is the band leader of this talented group. His is a veteran in the DMV music scene and has been performing for over 15 years. He commands with poise and grace reading the crowd to ensure the energy is maintained throughout each performance.

Ron (Drums)

Ron is hands down one of the best drummers in the DMV! He has the ear of a master musician and controls the tempo with ease. He will have you tapping/stomping your whether your sitting at a table or on the dance floor killing it.

George (Vocals)

George is the master of impersonation and improvisation. You will do a double take when he sings some of your favorite artist’s tracks! Additionally, he can serve as your MC for the night ensuring that the schedule of events runs smoothly.
