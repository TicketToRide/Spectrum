SPECTRUM 

Awesome Events Every Time. 

R&B Recording Artist and Vocal Impersonator George Jamison, Latin Singer/Songwriter Ramon Delacruz and Internationally Renowned Entertainer Natalya Altra

The Dream Team Line-Up of SPECTRUM.

A band is only as good as its ingredients and SPECTRUM’S ingredients are Top Notch!

They always deliver amazing performances by dazzling and interacting with your guests throughout the party. Bandleader Ben Kaufman jams on the keyboard and bass guitar while making sure your celebration runs flawlessly.  The band’s young gun Scott Rosendall shreds the Les Paul guitar while top-notch musicians Matt Shea and Matt Ellis jam on multiple instruments. Drummer Ron Byrd, Jr. rounds out the back line to make sure you feel the music as well as you hear it.

SPECTRUM hails from our Nation’s Capital, but are well known and highly respected from Maine to Miami Beach.

SPECTRUM’s talent is rivaled, but unmatched. They are among the elite bands leading the area’s music industry. SPECTRUM remains at the top of their game and like a fine wine, they only get better with time.

Your guests will love SPECTRUM at corporate parties, Galas and events– they will have your friends and family on the dance floor all night!
